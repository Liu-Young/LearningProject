Quality Control & Collection

Monitoring

After the departure of customers, Reca will:

Sort out all the sales confirmations, make clear all the information about the orders goods with the sellers, and make and send order list and fi nancial report to customers for confirmation.Contents in the order list include names of sellers, goods descriptions, specifications, quantity, prices, weight, volume, photos of commodities, production terms.

In the financial reports, it indicates budgets for payment of the orders, commission to Reca, transportation charges and other expenses.

Keep monitoring sellers to complete the orders on time. If any problems occur during the production, Reca will assist with communication between customers and sellers.

Inspection and Collection

After receiving the payment from customers, Reca will arrange quality controllers to inspect the produced goods.

In the procedure of inspecting:

Reca's professional quality controllers will make sure all details of completed products, such as models, item numbers, quantity, color, sizes, etc. are exactly the same as customer's requirement, and control the quality of the products is flawless.

When the goods are correct and approved, Reca will collect and store them at Reca's warehouse.

If the goods fail to meet the customer's requirements during inspection, Reca will request the sellers to replace or reproduce the defective goods. Reca will never accept the goods that fail the inspection requirements.

Reca always inspect goods seriously and strictly in order to protect customers' interests.

After collecting goods, Reca will send inspection reports with photos to the customers.

Customs Clearance and Transportation

Once the goods are collected and stored in Reca's warehouse, Reca's staff will measure the weight and dimension of each goods, to make sure all information of the goods is accurate.

When all of above processes have been completed to our satisfaction, Reca will book containers and vessel space with a shipping company, and then arrange trucking, containers loading and customs clearance for exportation from China.

At the end of the container loading, Reca's staff will take photos of loaded container and the seal on the container's doors, in order to show customers the status of the loaded container.

Within 3 working days after the container loading, Reca will send customers a loading list that includes complete and accurate information for each of the loaded goods.

After delivery, Reca will apply for the bill of lading based on the consignee details provided by customers. Before the containers arrive at the destination port, Reca will send customers packing list, commercial invoice, and telex release or original bills of lading for containers.

If it is necessary, Reca can make the customs clearance of the purchased goods in Russian Customs for customers, and deliver the goods to customers’ houses in any city in Russia.
