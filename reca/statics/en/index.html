Reca-International Company

Reca International Company Limited (hereinafter referred to as Reca) is a multi-functioned company, which is located in Guangzhou and began its business in 2001. Reca is an international trade & logistics company whose business and services cover ordering assistance, trade, customs clearance, bonded warehousing and logistics. During the past 10 years, Reca has served numerous customers from different countries. Reca's years of efforts and practices have brought Reca rich experiences, making it a professional foreign-trade company with a complete operating system.
