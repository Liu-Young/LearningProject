Logistics and Customs Clearance

Reca is one of the largest customs clearance enterprises in Guangzhou with customs clearance rate which reaches 6000 TEUs per month and up to and above 8000 TEUs during peak periods.

Reca provides customers with the following services:

    international shipping services for FCL (Full Container Load) and LCL (Less Container Load) goods

    international air cargo

    goods collection

    bonded warehousing at port of Guangzhou

    customs inspection

    brokers of customs clearance

    quality inspection & quarantine in customs

    Chinese licenses & certificates for importation & exportation

    Organizing multi-transportation ways for different types of goods.

The ideal logistics routes are to ports in Russia, Southeast Asia, West Africa, Middle East, the Northern America from basic ports in China.

Reca can arrange transportation services from China to the basic ports all around the world. Based on customers’ special requirements and actual situation of goods, Reca can choose and suggest the best logistics scheme for customers.
